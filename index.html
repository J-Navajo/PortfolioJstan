About me

Ya'ah'teeh (Greetings in Navajo language) My name is Jennifer Stanley. You are welcome to call me "J". I am from Kayenta, Az, located on the Navajo Reservation, just 21-22 miles south of the Utah/Arizona border. I come from a big family of 7 siblings with lots of cousins and nieces and nephews. I am somewhat in the middle. I moved to Phoenix, Az about 4 years ago after working in New Mexico for a Tribal College building their online program and Dual Credit department.

After high school I attended the University of Arizona and earned my Bachelor of Science in Agriculture Technology Management. I received my Masters in Agriculture Education and Educational Technology also from the University of Arizona. Can you tell I didn't want to leave UofA? Now I am back again attending the Coding Bootcamp course.

Some of my interests include but not limited to; painting, drawing, spending time with family/friends, sports, being active, going for a spontaneous drive, and designing. Although I am horrible at drawing and designing I keep at it because I beleive in practice makes perfect.

Currently, I work for the devil (ASU), as an Instructional Designer for the Engineering department. I enjoy building and designing online courses with professors when they're willing and excited to work together. Although I work for ASU I am a diehard wildcats fan. I still follow the UofA basketball as much as time will allow. I've been called a traitor by friends, family, and coworkers (all in good fun). Thank you for stopping by my page.
